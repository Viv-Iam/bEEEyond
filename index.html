Some frequently asked questions.

What is bEEEyond Mentorship?

bEEEyond Mentorship is a program that empowers the next generation of African innovators by helping them grow through Engagement, Expertise, and Exposure giving them real, practical skills and opportunities to succeed.

How long does the mentorship last?

bEEEyond mentorship lasts 6 months and commitment is 2 hours each month.

Why should I join bEEEyond Mentorship?

If you want a competitive edge that helps you tap into exciting tech opportunities, bEEEyond Mentorship is the place to be.

How can I get involved with bEEEyond?

Click the Get Involved button at the top or bottom of our site and let us know if you’d like to join as a mentor, mentee, or advisor.

I don’t have a tech background can I still benefit from bEEEyond mentorship?

Yes, if you’re interested in learning and growing in tech. Our focus is on helping people enter and advance in the tech space, so you’ll benefit most if you want to upskill in this field.

Am I guaranteed opportunities offered through mentorship?

No, while we share various opportunities, access to them is not guaranteed.
